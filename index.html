Intro

Pita Kebab is a buffet restaurant that is located in Antwerp.

Mission

Pita Kebabs mission is thinking about the quality of foods with healthy for our customers. To compete with other restaurants we will:


1. Threat our customers as kings.
2. Think about the healthy and the best taste foods for our customers.
3. Create more type of foods with different, shapes.
4. Keep good relations with customers.
5. Permanently remain the operation as legal, ethical, social responsible and environment behavior.

Work


Pita Kebab is a family owned and operated dine-in, take-out, and delivery restaurant located in Borgerhout, Antwerp. Some of our specialties are Greek Food/ Mediterranean and Middle Eastern cuisine. We serve the area of Borgerhout, Merksem, Kiel, Hoboken, and Antwerpen-zuid. Our signature dishes include homemade hummus dip, mixed grill kebab platter, fresh Greek salads, braised lamb shank, and lamb gyro, the best around.

About

Owner and chef Badr is proud to bring something entirely new to the community. The native Moroccan who grew up with the Mediterranean Sea as his backyard. Having a close-knit family and the strong influence of European culture, he learned to appreciate customs, superb food and the importance of having a meal together. That tradition is brought to the South shore area and his passion for Mediterranean cuisine is reflected in every meal he creates. . Our chef and his caring and committed staff will ensure you have a fantastic experience with us

Our restaurant is available to cater private events: weddings, business lunches, dinners, cocktail receptions, and more. We would love to discuss how to be a part of your next event.
